Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

Registration

Q:   How to register?

At the top of the website after our restaurant logoand name, there are a "Registration" option.
You need to create your own username and password and some personal detail.
Then, press "Registration"button
*Customer are required to register first before order food

Login

Q:   How to login?

After register successful, it will proceed to login by using username and password that created by yourself
*Please remember your Username and Password.

Order

Q:   How to order the food?

You need to click the "Cart" button on top and select the food you like.
If you want to submit your order, please press"Submit Order"button,then you will proceed to payment.
If you want to cancel all the order and choose again, please press"Cancel Order"button

!!!  Please check your order before submit and payment.The order will not change after payment done
*Registration and Login first before order

Contact

Q:   What is the function of Contact?

It can be used to contact us when you faced problem that cannot be resolved by FAQ page
You can use email, phone number or fill in the form to contact us

Payment

Q:   How to do the payment?

After you select food and enter "Submit".You will directly proceed to the Payment page.
You are required to select the payment option:
(E-WALLET/CREDIT/CASH ON DELIVERY)


Delivery

Q:   How select the delivery method?

You can select the delivery method at payment page before complete payment.
The delivery fee will calculate with the total price of order.

Events and Discount

Q:   How to know when the event of promotion?

You can click "Event" on the top and you will see our opening and closing time everyday except some certain day.
The event of promotion can see at the below of the opening and closing time.
**Any extra special events will be posted here

Reservation

Q:   How to make reservation?

You can click "Reservation" on the top and you will need to fill in your personal detail.
You can choose the date you want for reservation, number of people and type of reservation.
**if have any extra instruction, you can write in the textbox, then press "Submit"
