Origin and history of Dunhuang Flying Sky:

Call the gods flying in the air flying as flying heavens, while China's Taoism calls the mythical figures of ascension immortals, later combined to develop into Dunhuang Flying Sky, to the flying gods painted in the Dunhuang grottoes. Earliest history can be traced back to the Sixteen Kingdoms of Northern Liang to the Northern Wei Period, later continued to innovate and develop, and gradually declined in the Yuan Dynasty.
