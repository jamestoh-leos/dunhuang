Our Story Begins In The East.

Our Story

Our Background

Our Story Begins In The East. During the covid era, Dun Huang Chinese Cuisine was founded. Owner of Dun Huang Chinese Cuisine Chef Jerry came up with this concept in 2015 after turning down his first employment offer in China. In light of it, Jerry returned to Penang is to begin this eatery in the same year.Our recipe originated in Guang Dong, which is also where we developed our initial recipe. This brand-new Guang Dong restaurant pays homage to our roots in a lively, fascinating manner with its stunning patterns and vibrant colors.

Our Mission & Vision

Our goal is to make our clients happy and smile while enjoying our food. We work hard to offer our customers fresh, high-quality cuisine every day. In addition, we want to give our clients excellent service. Our long-term goal is to grow our company internationally and to expand our global branch network.

Our Services

We offer delivery service in addition to dine-in service. Both ways are acceptable for customers to enjoy our meals. One of our restaurant's biggest advantages is that orders may be placed and meals delivered within 30 minutes.
